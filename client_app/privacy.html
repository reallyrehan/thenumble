Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2025

Thank you for playing and visiting The Numble ("the Site"/"the Game").

Data Collection

This is a client-only application. All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers.

The following data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Your game progress and guess history
  • Your personal statistics (games played, wins, etc.)
  • Your dark mode preference
This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server.
